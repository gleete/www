About My Work

Hey there. This is my personal website. I made it from scratch, no special editors (dreamweaver) or programs (flash) here. This website is built entirely in HTML5, CSS3, and javascript. In order to ensure continuity between mobile, tablet, and desktop views I have kept most of the site simple.

Some of my previous employment includes: CACI at the Rome Air Force Research Lab, iCardiac Technologies, Apple Inc, and most recently Tagged in San Francisco.

Web Design

I know this site looks a lot different than a traditional website. Yet, that was one of the goals of my design when creating it. I am using only sans serif fonts. The paragraph you are reading right now is using Futura, the contentbox header is Manhattan Sans, and the heading to the left is Bebas Neue. In order to simplfy the initial information someone seeks at this home page, I have put my paragraphs to display:none and they ease out using jQuery when clicking the assigned button on the vertical navigation bar to the left.
